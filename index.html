cherry peak

Opened in 2014, Cherry Peak is Utah's newest ski arena. It is located just 15 miles from Logan, near the Idaho border. The family-owned, nearly 400-acre resort, features three triple chairlifts, a comprahensive snowmaking infrastructure and a 1.25 mile-long run. The resort recently opened a three-story day Grand Lodge that hosts events year-round
